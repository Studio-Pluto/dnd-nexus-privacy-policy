# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 11, 2026

Pluto.Studio ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application **DnD Nexus: Ultimate Guide** (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and disclosure when you use our App.

By downloading and using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

---

## 1. No Personal Data Collection
We value your privacy above all else. **DnD Nexus: Ultimate Guide does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII).** * We do not require account creation, registration, or login credentials.
* We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, or device identifiers.
* The App does not contain any health-related, financial, or medical content or data tracking.

---

## 2. Permissions & Internet Usage
The App requires internet access to function properly. This permission is used strictly for the following technical reasons:
* **Global Search Functionalilty:** To fetch and query online handbook resources and database entries dynamically.
* **Ad Delivery:** To load advertisements served by third-party ad networks.
* **In-App Purchases:** To validate transactions via the Google Play Billing API when removing advertisements.

No user-generated search queries are saved or associated with your identity.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services & Advertisements
The App displays interstitial advertisements provided by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob) to fund development. These third-party networks may use tracking technologies (such as cookies, SDKs, or mobile advertising IDs) to serve relevant non-personalized or personalized ads based on your general device settings, subject to their respective privacy policies.

You can completely opt out of ad tracking through your mobile device's native operating system settings (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).

---

## 4. In-App Purchases (Ad Removal)
The App offers a single-tier In-App Purchase (IAP) to remove interstitial advertisements. All transactions and financial details are processed securely and entirely by Google Play Billing Services. We do not have access to, nor do we store, your credit card information, bank account details, or billing addresses.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy
Because **DnD Nexus: Ultimate Guide** does not collect any personal information, it is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) frameworks regarding minors. We do not knowingly collect or solicit data from anyone under the age of 13.
